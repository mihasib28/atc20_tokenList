What is AtlantisChain?

AtlantisChain is a U.S.-designed Layer-ZERO public blockchain engineered for real-time commerce, financial infrastructure, and public services. Powered by the innovative Proof of Light (PoL) consensus algorithm, the network delivers up to 3,600,000 transactions per second while maintaining a block time and finality of just 0.031 seconds.

Built for Global Scale

AtlantisChain has been extensively reported by more than 400 media outlets throughout the United States. The network is designed to support stablecoins, real-world assets (RWAs), enterprise payments, and large-scale decentralized applications requiring predictable performance and extremely low fees.

AI-Powered & Quantum-Resistant

AtlantisChain is described as the world's first AI-powered and quantum-resistant blockchain. Its architecture focuses on security, commercial scalability, and real-time settlement while maintaining fixed near-zero transaction fees.

Atlantis Time

AtlantisChain introduced Atlantis Time, a proprietary blockchain time system that runs parallel to Earth Time while operating independently within the network architecture.

Atlantis Wallet & QuantumLock™

ATC-20 assets are secured through Atlantis Wallet, protected by the Atlantis QuantumLock™ security framework designed to defend against advanced cyber threats and future quantum-era attack vectors.

Protected Intellectual Property

AtlantisChain states that its intellectual property is protected by a USPTO-registered trademark under the Atlantis Coin® brand, giving the ecosystem a unique position within the blockchain industry.

Atlantis Coin® (ATC)

Atlantis Coin® serves as the native utility token powering the entire AtlantisChain ecosystem. It facilitates payments, transfers, token operations, and network activity throughout the platform.

Future Vision

Built by an MIT-rooted U.S. team, AtlantisChain aims to provide governments, institutions, enterprises, and individuals with scalable Web3 infrastructure capable of supporting real-time global commerce and digital finance.
