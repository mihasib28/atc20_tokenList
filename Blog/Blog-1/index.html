What is ATC20 Direct?

ATC20 Direct is the dedicated, official platform for acquiring assets built on the ATC-20 standard on AtlantisChain. It functions as a secure, bank-grade portal designed to simplify the process of purchasing and managing these unique tokens. The platform is overseen by the Atlantis Kingdom Foundation (a U.S. nonprofit), emphasizing compliance and security at every step.

The power of AtlantisChain

Built for speed and scale, AtlantisChain delivers near-instant finality and minimal transaction costs. These attributes make the chain an attractive home for stablecoins, tokenized assets, and high-frequency applications.

Exclusive access to key assets

ATC20 Direct is the initial supply portal for core stablecoins like USDA and CNYC, and also provides ATC-20 wrappers for major cryptos such as BTC, ETH, BNB, and SOL — enabling holders to benefit from AtlantisChain's speed and low cost.

Security & simple process

Users can fund accounts with fiat via approved gateways or deposits in supported cryptocurrencies. After purchase and approval, assets are credited to your ATC20 Direct account or withdrawn to Atlantis Wallet.
